What "Military-Friendly Listing" Actually Means

  • VA loan acceptable: seller is open to VA buyers and has not written "VA financing not accepted" or similar exclusions. Occasionally sellers exclude VA from accepted financing types; military buyers filter these out upfront.
  • MPR-clean: roof, HVAC, plumbing, electrical all in functional condition. A VA appraisal requires Minimum Property Requirements — listings with deferred-maintenance red flags create costly repair-credit negotiations at closing.
  • Price within BAH bracket: total monthly payment (P&I + taxes + insurance + HOA) fits inside your BAH after 2026 rate adjustments.
  • Gate commute under 30 minutes: verified by actual drive time at shift-change rush hour, not just map estimate.
  • Parcel-zoned to an acceptable school cluster: verified against county zoning maps, not listing agent descriptions.
