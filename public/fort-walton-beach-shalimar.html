Fort Walton Beach & Shalimar — Eglin West Gate Housing for Military Families

The primary city adjacent to Eglin AFB's West Gate, plus the small town of Shalimar immediately east of the base. Shortest commute to the 96th Test Wing, 53rd Wing, and AFRL Munitions Directorate. Beach access, Okaloosa schools, and a broad mix of price points.

Fort Walton Beach (FWB) is the primary city of western Okaloosa County and wraps around the west and south edges of Eglin AFB. Shalimar is a small incorporated town immediately east of FWB, essentially inside the Eglin gate loop. Together they are the default west-side choice for Eglin personnel assigned to the 96th Test Wing, 53rd Wing, AFRL/RW, and other west-Eglin tenants, and they are also a strong pick for Hurlburt Field commuters who want a shorter drive than Navarre. FWB offers a genuine mix of price points — from E-4/E-5-friendly inventory in the $275K-$375K range to established waterfront homes over $800K — which makes it one of the most flexible communities in the FL023 Fort Walton Beach MHA.

Why Fort Walton Beach / Shalimar Work for Military Buyers

Three draws: commute, price flexibility, and schools. For Eglin West Gate and Hurlburt Field commuters, FWB and Shalimar deliver some of the shortest drives in the Panhandle — 5-15 minutes depending on neighborhood. Price flexibility is real: this is one of very few communities in the FL023 MHA where E-4 to E-5 BAH can support a move-in-ready 3BR home. Okaloosa schools are strong, with Fort Walton Beach High and Choctawhatchee High both well-regarded, and Choctawhatchee's IB program attracting military-connected families specifically. The trade-off: FWB lacks the prestige new-construction feel of Niceville's Bluewater Bay and the beach lifestyle of Destin.

Top Fort Walton Beach / Shalimar Sub-Neighborhoods

Downtown / Cinco Bayou area

Older established neighborhoods along Cinco Bayou. Mix of mid-century and renovated homes. $285K-$425K. Strong E-5 to E-6 fit with some waterfront opportunity.

Shalimar proper

Immediately east of Eglin, centered on Poquito Bayou. Mid-century homes, tight-knit town. $325K-$525K. Shortest commute for Eglin East-Gate adjacent work.

Ocean City / Cinco Bayou West

North-of-US-98 FWB neighborhoods. Mix of 1970s-90s inventory and newer infill. $275K-$400K. Best starter-home value.

Waterfront Choctawhatchee Bay

Premium bayfront streets in FWB and Shalimar. $525K-$950K+. Waterfront, some with boat docks. Best O-4+ fit.

Okaloosa Island

Gulf-front barrier island condos across the Destin Bridge. $300K-$650K. Strong short-term rental potential. HOA considerations apply.

BAH and Purchase Price

FL023 Fort Walton Beach MHA 2026 monthly BAH with dependents: E-5 $2,433, E-6 $2,526, E-7 $2,841, O-3 $3,399, O-5 $3,612. In FWB/Shalimar:

• E-4 BAH ($2,340) supports $350K-$370K — a meaningful slice of the FWB starter-home market.

• E-5 and E-6 BAH comfortably cover the mainstream family-home inventory.

• O-3 and above BAH reaches into waterfront and Shalimar premium tiers.

Schools for FWB / Shalimar Families

Okaloosa County Schools. Elementary options include Elliot Point, Edge, and Shalimar Elementary. Middle schools include Max Bruner Jr Middle and Meigs Middle. The two primary high schools are Fort Walton Beach High and Choctawhatchee High (in Shalimar). Choctawhatchee's IB program is a specific draw for military-connected families. The district honors the Interstate Compact for Educational Opportunity for Military Children.

Hurricane and Flood Considerations

Bayfront and Okaloosa Island inventory can sit in Zone AE or VE with flood premiums $1,000-$3,500/year. Most interior FWB and Shalimar streets are Zone X with minimal exposure. Wind insurance in the Fort Walton Beach area is higher than inland Crestview but lower than waterfront Destin. Pull FEMA flood determination and wind mitigation form on every property before offer.

Frequently Asked Questions

How close is Fort Walton Beach to Eglin AFB?

The Eglin AFB West Gate is 5-15 minutes from most Fort Walton Beach and Shalimar neighborhoods via Eglin Boulevard and SR-189. Hurlburt Field is 10-15 minutes via US-98. For military personnel assigned to the 96th Test Wing, 53rd Wing, or AFRL Munitions Directorate at Eglin's west side, FWB and Shalimar are the shortest commutes.

What BAH rate applies to Fort Walton Beach and Shalimar?

Both are in the Fort Walton Beach MHA (FL023). 2026 monthly rates with dependents: E-5 $2,433, E-6 $2,526, E-7 $2,841, E-8 $3,189, O-3 $3,399, O-4 $3,528, O-5 $3,612. FL023 is one of the higher-BAH MHAs in the Southeast because beach-area Destin pricing drives the rate.

What schools serve Fort Walton Beach and Shalimar?

Okaloosa County Schools. Fort Walton Beach High School and Choctawhatchee High School (in Shalimar) are the primary comprehensive high schools. Both earn solid Florida grades. Choctawhatchee has strong IB and AP pathways and a meaningful military-connected student population. The district honors the Interstate Compact for Educational Opportunity for Military Children.

Fort Walton Beach vs Niceville for an Eglin family?

Fort Walton Beach wins on commute if you work at Eglin West Gate (5-10 min vs Niceville's 15-20 to East Gate) or Hurlburt. Niceville wins on schools (Niceville High consistently tops Okaloosa rankings), newer housing stock in Bluewater Bay, and proximity to Destin. FWB is more affordable across most of its inventory.

Can I afford Fort Walton Beach on E-5 or E-6 BAH?

Yes, comfortably. FL023 E-5 BAH ($2,433) supports $360K+ in FWB, which covers most 3BR inventory. E-6 opens up the full family-home market including newer construction. This is one of the strongest BAH-to-home-price pairings in the FL023 MHA for junior enlisted families.
