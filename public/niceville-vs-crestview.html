Niceville vs Crestview — Which Okaloosa Town Fits Your PCS?

Niceville is the premium school pick for Eglin and Hurlburt families. Crestview is the budget pick that also works for Duke Field and 7th SFG. Here's the head-to-head.

If you're PCSing to Eglin AFB, Duke Field, or even Hurlburt Field, Niceville and Crestview are the two Okaloosa County towns you'll consider most often. Both serve military families. Both have active military liaisons in their schools. They are different enough in culture, schools, and price that the right answer depends heavily on rank, family size, and commute.

School Clusters

  • Niceville: Niceville High, Ruckel Middle, Bluewater Elementary / Edge Elementary / Plew Elementary. Niceville High is A-rated consistently and has the strongest AP catalog in Okaloosa County. Strong choice for rigor-prioritizing families.
  • Crestview: Crestview High, Shoal River Middle, Riverside Elementary / Walker Elementary / Bob Sikes Elementary. Rated B / B+ district-wide. Solid vocational tracks and NJROTC. Mid-tier overall.

Niceville is clearly the stronger school play. If schools are the top priority, Niceville wins.

Commute Times

  • Niceville to Eglin AFB east gate: 10 minutes via SR-85 or John Sims Parkway.
  • Niceville to Hurlburt Field: 20-30 minutes via Mid-Bay Bridge (toll) or the free coastal route.
  • Niceville to Duke Field: 25 minutes via SR-85.
  • Crestview to Eglin AFB north gate: 20-25 minutes via SR-85 south.
  • Crestview to Duke Field: 10-15 minutes via SR-85 — shortest commute to Duke Field of any practical buy zone.
  • Crestview to Hurlburt Field: 30-40 minutes. Too far to be practical.

Housing Price

Crestview is 25-40% cheaper per square foot than comparable Niceville inventory. A 2,400-sf 4-bed / 3-bath in Niceville's Bluewater Bay runs $525K-625K. A comparable home in Crestview off PJ Adams Parkway or Brookmeade Drive runs $350K-$425K. Crestview's acreage properties in Laurel Hill and Holt are significantly cheaper than Niceville's suburban equivalents.

Community Culture

  • Niceville: higher-income, officer-and-senior-enlisted dominant, suburban family community. Strong HOAs in Bluewater Bay and Rocky Bayou. Active boating / waterfront culture on Choctawhatchee Bay.
  • Crestview: mixed-income, E-5 through E-7 dominant, rural and small-town in character. Less HOA overhead, more DIY / outdoor / rural culture. Hunting and outdoor-gear communities are strong.

Base-by-Base Recommendation

  • Eglin AFB family, schools priority: Niceville.
  • Eglin AFB family, budget priority: Crestview (plus longer commute) or Fort Walton Beach (shorter commute, comparable price).
  • Duke Field family: Crestview by default. Niceville only if you're willing to drive 25 minutes and want the schools.
  • Hurlburt family: Niceville if you can afford it; Navarre if you want Santa Rosa schools; Mary Esther if you want the shortest commute.
  • 7th SFG Green Berets at Eglin: Crestview, Baker, or Laurel Hill for acreage and privacy.

Who Picks Niceville

  • Eglin officers and senior enlisted on 3+ year tours
  • 33rd FW F-35A instructor pilots and 96th TW permanent party
  • Families with school-age kids prioritizing Niceville High
  • Boating and waterfront-lifestyle households

Who Picks Crestview

  • Duke Field 919th SOW full-time personnel (ART, AGR, 2nd SOS)
  • 7th SFG Green Berets wanting acreage and privacy
  • E-5 through E-7 Eglin families prioritizing budget
  • Families wanting rural Okaloosa lifestyle and lower HOA overhead

Frequently Asked Questions

Which has better schools, Niceville or Crestview?

Niceville by a significant margin. Niceville High has the strongest AP catalog in Okaloosa County and consistently earns A grades. Crestview schools are B / B+ district-wide — solid but not at Niceville's level.

How much cheaper is Crestview than Niceville?

Typically 25-40% cheaper per square foot. A 2,400 sf 4-bed in Niceville's Bluewater Bay runs $525K-625K; comparable Crestview runs $350K-425K. Crestview is where E-5 through E-7 Eglin families get the most house for BAH.

Is Crestview close enough to Eglin to commute?

Yes — 20-25 minutes via SR-85 to the Eglin north gate. Acceptable daily commute for families prioritizing budget over travel time. 96th TW personnel assigned to the main-base south-side should add 5-10 minutes.

Which town works better for Duke Field?

Crestview — by far. Duke Field is 10-15 minutes from Crestview via SR-85. Niceville is 25 minutes. Crestview is the default Duke Field buy zone.

Is Niceville worth the premium over Crestview for military families?

Depends on priorities. If schools are top priority or you're an officer/senior enlisted with the BAH to absorb the premium, Niceville wins. If budget is the top priority or you're a junior enlisted family or 7th SFG Green Beret wanting acreage, Crestview wins.
