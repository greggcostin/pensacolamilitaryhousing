Pace, FL — The Smart Value Play for NAS Whiting Field & Northern Pensacola Commanders

A-rated Santa Rosa schools, bigger lots, newer construction, and the best BAH-to-square-foot ratio in the Pensacola MHA.

If your orders drop you at NAS Whiting Field (or your spouse works at NAS Pensacola and you want more house for the money), Pace is the answer most civilian Realtors miss. 20 minutes north of downtown Pensacola, Pace gives you the exact same A-rated Santa Rosa County school district as Gulf Breeze at roughly 25-35% lower cost per square foot, with newer construction and bigger yards. It is the highest-value play in the Pensacola MHA for E-5 through O-3 families.

Commute to NAS Whiting Field: 15 min via Hwy 90
Commute to NAS Pensacola: 30-35 min
Commute to Corry Station: 25 min
School District: Santa Rosa County (A-rated)
BAH MHA: FL064 — Pensacola
Median home price (2026): $310,000–$395,000
Typical VA loan fit: E-4 and above
Lot sizes: Typically 0.25-0.5 acre+

Why Pace Wins on BAH Math

Pace gives you three things that are hard to find together in Pensacola: newer construction (lots of 2015-2024 builds), Santa Rosa County A-rated schools, and entry prices that fit an E-4 or E-5 BAH. A 3-bedroom, 2-bath, 1,800 sqft newer build in Stonebrook or Ashley Plantation typically trades $310K–$360K — well inside an E-5 VA loan budget. If your spouse is a Whiting Field instructor or student aviator, the 15-minute commute is unbeatable.

Top Pace Sub-Neighborhoods

Stonebrook

Newer master-planned community, HOA-managed, family-heavy, playgrounds and walking paths. Homes $325K-$425K.

Ashley Plantation

Larger estate-style lots, slightly higher price point ($375K-$500K). Strong fit for O-3 and above.

Woodbine / Thornberry

Established 2000s-era neighborhoods. Excellent VA-loan values in $275K-$340K range.

Rural north Pace (past Hwy 90)

Acreage properties, equestrian, 1-5 acre lots. For families wanting land.

Schools — Why Pace Schools are a Best-Kept Secret

Pace High School consistently earns an A grade and has a well-regarded AP pathway and competitive athletics. Pace Elementary, Dixon Primary, and Sims Middle are all A-rated. The district shares the same military-family support infrastructure as Gulf Breeze and Navarre — Interstate Compact honored, records transfer streamlined, and a growing population of NAS Whiting Field student-naval-aviator families.

NAS Whiting Field Proximity — The Pace Advantage

NAS Whiting Field is the Navy's primary helicopter training base and a major T-6 fixed-wing training hub. Pace sits 15 minutes from Whiting's main gate via Hwy 90 — the shortest reliable commute of any A-rated-school neighborhood in the area. For a student naval aviator or Whiting instructor with a 0500 brief, Pace is objectively the best call. Milton is closer but school ratings are lower; Gulf Breeze has the same schools but 40-minute commute.

Flood, Hurricane, and Insurance in Pace

Pace is inland — almost the entire area is Zone X (preferred flood zone). Flood insurance is minimal or optional for most properties, saving $1,500-$3,000/year versus Gulf Breeze or Navarre Beach. Wind insurance is also typically 20-30% cheaper than coastal equivalents. This is a meaningful monthly payment difference for BAH-constrained families.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Pace better than Gulf Breeze for NAS Whiting Field?

Yes. Pace is 15 minutes to Whiting Field; Gulf Breeze is 40 minutes. Same A-rated Santa Rosa County schools, significantly lower cost per square foot, and lower insurance. For Whiting Field families, Pace is the correct pick almost every time.

What BAH do I need to buy in Pace?

E-4 BAH ($1,700-$1,850 with deps) supports entry-level Pace homes in the $250K-$290K range. E-5 and E-6 BAH puts Stonebrook and Woodbine firmly in play at $310K-$360K. A VA loan with zero down is the standard play.

Are Pace schools really A-rated?

Yes. Pace High, Pace Elementary, Dixon Primary, and Sims Middle are all A-rated under Florida DOE grading. Same Santa Rosa County district as Gulf Breeze and Navarre.

Is Pace or Milton closer to NAS Whiting Field?

Milton is 8-10 minutes closer, but Pace schools are materially stronger. For single service members, Milton is fine. For families with school-age kids, Pace's schools are worth the extra 5-7 minutes.
