Navarre, FL — Affordable Military Housing Between Hurlburt Field and NAS Pensacola

Florida's "Best Kept Secret" — family-friendly, beach-adjacent, A-rated Santa Rosa County schools, and 15-25% cheaper per square foot than Gulf Breeze.

Navarre sits right in the sweet spot between Hurlburt Field (25 minutes east) and NAS Pensacola (35 minutes west), and it is hands-down the best value for military families who want A-rated schools, a beach lifestyle, and a mortgage that fits comfortably inside their BAH. I've placed AFSOC aviators, NAS Pensacola instructors, and Eglin engineers in Navarre — it works for all three. Here is the straight scoop.

Commute to Hurlburt Field: 25 min via US-98
Commute to NAS Pensacola: 35 min
Commute to Eglin AFB: 30 min
School District: Santa Rosa County (A-rated)
BAH MHA: FL064 — Pensacola (Navarre side)
Median home price (2026): $340,000–$425,000
Typical VA loan fit: E-5 and above, O-1 and above
Beach access: Navarre Beach — 5 min

Why Navarre is the BAH-Friendliest Family Pick

The Navarre math is simple: same A-rated Santa Rosa County school district as Gulf Breeze, meaningfully lower cost per square foot, and an easier commute if you are assigned to Hurlburt Field or Eglin AFB. A 3-bedroom move-in-ready home in Holley By The Sea or Biscayne Pointe regularly trades in the $340K–$410K range — which fits an E-5 to E-7 BAH with room for taxes and insurance. For AFSOC (Hurlburt) and 7th SFG / 1st SOW families, Navarre is the default choice.

Top Navarre Sub-Neighborhoods

Holley By The Sea

The largest master-planned community in Navarre with private clubhouse, pools, tennis, and sound access. Homes run $375K–$550K. Excellent for O-3 and above or dual-income E-7+ families.

Biscayne Pointe & The Estuary

Waterfront and water-view, newer construction. Higher insurance but premium lifestyle.

East Bay / Navarre Beach

On the barrier island. Second-home and vacation-rental territory; limited for primary-residence military buyers due to insurance costs.

Villa Sabine / Navarre proper

Older established neighborhoods, $300K–$400K, best VA loan value zones.

BAH-to-Home-Price Match for Navarre

E-5 BAH (~$1,800–$1,950) comfortably supports $275K–$310K in Navarre, which opens up Villa Sabine and parts of Navarre proper. E-6 to E-7 BAH supports $310K–$370K — Holley By The Sea entry-level and Biscayne Pointe become accessible. O-1E to O-3 BAH unlocks the full Holley By The Sea and Estuary inventory.

Schools and Military Family Support

Navarre-zoned schools (Holley-Navarre Primary, Holley-Navarre Intermediate, Holley-Navarre Middle, Navarre High School) are all A-rated and part of Santa Rosa County. Navarre High has a strong JROTC program and a high percentage of military-connected students. The district honors the Interstate Compact on Educational Opportunity for Military Children, which is non-negotiable for PCS families arriving mid-school-year.

Hurricane, Flood, and Insurance Factors

Navarre was hit hard by Hurricane Sally (2020) and Ivan (2004). Properties north of US-98 are generally in Zone X (preferred) with flood policies $400–$800/year. Properties south of US-98, on Navarre Beach, or along Santa Rosa Sound can be in Zone AE or VE — quotes can exceed $3,000/year. I pull the FEMA flood quote on every property before offer, period.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Navarre better than Gulf Breeze for Hurlburt Field?

Yes, unambiguously. Navarre is 25 minutes to Hurlburt Field vs 50+ minutes from Gulf Breeze. For AFSOC, 1st SOW, and 24th SOW families, Navarre is the correct choice. For NAS Pensacola families, Gulf Breeze is closer but Navarre offers better BAH value if you can accept the 35-minute commute.

What BAH do I need to buy in Navarre?

E-5 BAH supports entry-level Navarre homes in the $275K-$310K range. E-6 and above opens up Holley By The Sea and the broader market. O-3 and above has the full range. A VA loan with zero down is the standard play.

Are Navarre schools as good as Gulf Breeze schools?

They are in the same district (Santa Rosa County) and both earn Florida A grades. Gulf Breeze High edges Navarre High slightly on standardized test scores, but Navarre is ahead on the military-connected student population and JROTC resources.

How is hurricane insurance in Navarre?

Variable by location. North of US-98 is generally affordable ($400-$800/year flood, $1,800-$2,500/year wind). South of US-98 and on Navarre Beach, costs climb significantly. Always pull the quote before offer. I coordinate this on every Navarre transaction.
